About Us

At Matcha and You, we bring premium Japanese matcha to tea lovers around the globe. Our mission is to share the rich culture of matcha and the joy of mindful tea preparation.

Founded with passion for authenticity and wellness, we source our matcha from trusted Japanese farms, ensuring every sip delivers both taste and tradition.
